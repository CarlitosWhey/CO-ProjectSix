Instructions:

  1. Enter your preferred name.
  2. Write your message.
  3. Paste the URL of the image or GIF you'd like to use for the card.
  4. Click "Generate Card"
  5. Click the "Feature" button if you'd like to highlight your card(s) and click the "Delete" button if you'd like to remove them.
  6. Create as many cards as you'd like, share them with your friends/family, and most of all, ENJOY!
